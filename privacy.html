Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our Service, we collect the following:

  • Account Information: Your email address and hashed password.
  • Billing Information: Payment details (processed securely by PayPal/Stripe). We do not store your credit card numbers.
  • Usage Data: Text prompts used for AI generation and asset download history.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to operate and improve the Service. Specifically:

  • To process your 3D generation requests.
  • To manage your subscription and access rights.
  • To improve our AI models (e.g., using prompts to train for better "Roblox style" results).

3. Data Retention

Free Plan: Generated assets are automatically deleted after 72 hours.

Paid Plans: Assets are retained as long as your account is active.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) and secure servers to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@teamgoodmaker.team.
